How to use the Product Guide

A live view of what's sellable right now. Match a customer by aroma (terpenes), strength (THC), and effect. It updates from inventory on its own — nothing here can be changed by browsing.

Help a customer fast

  1. Ask what they want — sleepy, energized, a strain, high THC.
  2. Tap Indica, Sativa, or Hybrid up top to set the vibe.
  3. Search a strain or brand if they have one in mind.
  4. Tap THC or Total terps to sort strongest to the top.
  5. Tap any product for its full breakdown and similar items in stock.

Reading a product

  • Product — name, brand, and form (e.g. 1g Live Resin Vape).
  • Type — Indica / Sativa / Hybrid. A ≈ means it's our aroma-based estimate, not the maker's label.
  • THC — total THC %.
  • Other cannabinoids — CBD, CBG, CBN, etc. (letters on top, number below).
  • Top terpenes — the 3 biggest aromas and each one's share.
  • Terps — total terpene % (how loud the aroma is overall).
  • Qty — how much is in stock.

Filters (left side)

  • Location / Category — narrow by where it is or product type.
  • Terpene — pick one to show only products with it (then it sorts by most of it).
  • Group by dominant terpene — clusters products by their leading aroma.
  • THC slider — hide anything under a minimum %.
  • Cannabinoids present — show only items containing CBD, CBG, CBN, etc.
  • Clear all filters — reset everything.

Views

  • Customer View — makes everything big and high-contrast; turn the screen toward the customer with this on.
  • Table / Cards — Table suits a full screen; Cards suits a tablet or phone (it switches automatically on small screens).

Good to know

  • ≈ means estimate. When there's no lab terpene data, the effect type is our best aroma-based guess — say "similar profile," not a guarantee.
  • It's live. Low Qty means it's actually low.
  • It's read-only. Browse freely — you can't change anything.
